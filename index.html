Boundaries

Are you in the neighborhood?

FPNA represents residents across Thompson, 53rd, Cornell, Forest Lane, and the surrounding hillsides. The boundary is erratic in places — many residents are in unincorporated Multnomah County, others are in the City of Portland.

Forest Park Neighborhood — interactive map coming soon

Check your address

Enter your address and we'll tell you if you're inside the FPNA boundary, plus whether your home is in the City of Portland or unincorporated Multnomah County.

Boundary data is sourced from official Multnomah County and City of Portland records.

Join your neighborhood association.

Membership is free and open to residents, property owners, and business licensees within the FPNA boundary. You'll get occasional emails about meetings and neighborhood issues, and a voice in the work the association does on behalf of residents.

Welcoming statement

Forest Park Neighborhood welcomes everyone — all races, religions, countries of origin, sexual orientations, genders, and abilities. We embrace and respect one another first as neighbors, and we strive to look out for each other. Hate has no home here.
