Meetings

General meetings are open to all residents.

FPNA meets on the third Tuesday of each month at 7:00 PM at Skyline Memorial Gardens, 4101 NW Skyline Blvd — please use Gate 2, the northern gate. No meetings in February, July, or August. Meetings include association business, neighborhood updates, guest speakers, and time for resident questions.
