50th Anniversary planning

Help plan the August 22 celebration.

The 50th anniversary planning group meets bi-weekly through August to coordinate food, music, partner outreach, sponsorship, and the program for the day. Open to any FPNA member who wants to pitch in.

Contact board@forestparkneighbors.org to be added to the planning list. Meeting dates and Zoom links are sent to the planning group ahead of each session.

FPNA Elections

Nominations submitted by FPNA members are accepted. Candidates must be members of the association. Names, statements, and the time and place of voting are emailed to all FPNA members. Elections are administered by the District Four Coalition (D4C).

To submit nominations, join the association, or for additional information:
