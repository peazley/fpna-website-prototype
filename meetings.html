FPNA Elections

Nominations submitted by FPNA members are accepted. Candidates must be members of the association. Names, statements, and the time and place of voting are emailed to all FPNA members. Elections are administered by the District Four Coalition (D4C).

To submit nominations, join the association, or for additional information:

District Four Coalition
434 NW 6th Ave Suite 202, Portland, OR 97209
(971) 430-1001 · darlene@districtfourcoalition.org
