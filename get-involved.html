Get Involved

Be part of the neighborhood association.

Membership is free. You'll receive the Updates newsletter, meeting invitations, and a voice in the work the association does on behalf of residents.

Become a member

FPNA membership is open to any resident living within the FPNA boundary, free of charge. Members receive:

  • The monthly Updates newsletter
  • Invitations to general meetings and special events
  • Voting rights at the annual membership meeting
  • The ability to join committees and working groups
  • Direct line to the board on issues affecting residents

Not sure if you're in the boundary? Check your address on the about page.

Sign up

FPNA's official membership signup is hosted on AirTable through District Four Coalition. It takes about a minute.

Become a member →

You can also email president@forestparkneighbors.org to be added to the mailing list.

Volunteer

Ways to plug in.

FPNA's work is done by volunteer committees. Each committee has its own focus area and meets independently between general meetings.

Wildfire & Emergency Preparedness

Coordinating defensible-space education, evacuation planning, and partnership with PF&R on neighborhood fire readiness.

Get in touch →

Forest Park Stewardship

Liaison between FPNA and the Forest Park Conservancy, the City Parks bureau, and trail volunteer programs.

Get in touch →

Power & Tree Management

Working with PGE on tree-trimming and outage reduction, building on years of advocacy that's reduced power issues for residents.

Get in touch →

Land Use & Planning

Reviewing development proposals affecting the neighborhood and engaging with the city and county on land use decisions.

Communications

50th Anniversary

RSVP for the August 22 celebration.

Join us as we mark fifty years of FPNA. Food, music, neighborhood history, and a chance to meet the residents who've built this place. Free and open to all FPNA residents, with sponsor opportunities for local businesses and organizations.
